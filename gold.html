Understanding Gold Purity

Know what makes our gold special

24K Gold (99.9% Pure)

The purest form of gold, perfect for investment pieces and traditional jewelry. While softer than lower karats, it offers unmatched richness in color.

22K Gold (91.6% Pure)

The ideal balance between purity and durability. Contains small amounts of alloy to strengthen the gold while maintaining its beautiful color.

BIS Hallmarking

All our gold jewelry comes with Bureau of Indian Standards certification, guaranteeing the stated purity and protecting your investment.

Gold Jewelry FAQs

Answers to common questions about our gold collection

24K gold is 99.9% pure gold, while 22K gold contains 91.6% gold mixed with other metals for added strength. 24K has a richer color but is softer, making it ideal for simple designs and investment pieces. 22K is more durable for intricate jewelry while maintaining excellent gold content.

All our gold jewelry comes with BIS (Bureau of Indian Standards) hallmark certification, which includes:

  • BIS mark
  • Purity in carat (22K or 24K)
  • Assay center mark
  • Jeweler's identification mark
  • Year of marking

You can verify any piece at a certified assaying center.
